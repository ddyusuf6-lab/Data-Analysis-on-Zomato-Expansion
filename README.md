# Data-Analysis-on-Zomato-Expansion
This project analyzes the expansion strategy of Zomato using data analysis and visualization techniques. The objective is to uncover insights about market penetration, customer preferences, restaurant performance, and geographical trends to support data-driven business expansion decisions.

Project Objectives

Analyze restaurant distribution across different cities and countries.
Identify high-performing locations based on ratings, votes, and cuisines.
Study pricing patterns and affordability trends.
Evaluate customer preferences through ratings and reviews.
Provide strategic recommendations for future expansion.

Tools & Techniques Used

Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Charts & Graphs (Bar, Line, Pie, Map)
Conditional Formatting
VLOOKUP / XLOOKUP
Data Cleaning & Filtering
Dashboard Creation

Key Analysis Performed

City-wise Restaurant Distribution – Identified cities with the highest concentration of restaurants.
Cuisine Analysis – Determined the most popular cuisines across regions.
Rating & Votes Analysis – Studied correlation between ratings and customer engagement.
Cost for Two Analysis – Compared average pricing across cities.
Online Delivery & Table Booking Trends – Evaluated service availability patterns.
Country-wise Expansion Analysis – Reviewed international market presence.

Dashboard Features
Interactive filters (City, Country, Cuisine)
KPI indicators (Total Restaurants, Avg Rating, Avg Cost)
Visual trend comparisons

Top 10 cities and cuisines insights

Key Insights

Major metro cities show higher restaurant density and customer engagement.
Popular cuisines significantly influence ratings and votes.
Affordable pricing segments attract higher volume.
Online delivery availability positively impacts customer ratings.

#Conclusion

The analysis provides actionable insights into expansion opportunities for Zomato. By leveraging Excel-based data analytics, the project highlights potential high-growth markets and customer-driven trends that can guide strategic business decisions.
